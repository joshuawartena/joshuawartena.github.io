Overview
When I joined BELFOR, the digital marketing department was providing basic web services to franchise partners. I built it into a full-service internal agency — one that handles onboarding, website creation and support, Google Ads, SEO, and social media for 500+ franchise locations across a portfolio of international home services brands.
Campaign / dashboard screenshots
What I built
Starting from a small team handling reactive web requests, I grew the department to 10 digital marketing specialists with defined roles across SEO, paid search, social, and client onboarding. I developed training curricula covering WordPress, Google Ads, Umbraco, RavenTools, and SEO platforms — delivered to 500+ franchise clients. I also built out Google Ads as a formal department offering and expanded social media from organic posts to paid ad strategy.
Training materials / team
How it works
Each new franchise partner goes through a structured onboarding program — website setup, local SEO configuration, Google Business Profile, and ad campaign launch. Ongoing support covers campaign management, site updates, and quarterly strategy reviews. I present to franchise networks at national conventions and deliver webinars to keep partner knowledge current.
Outcome
Consistent year-over-year revenue growth across the department. BELFOR franchise partners now have access to a full-service digital marketing team that functions as an extension of their own business — without the overhead of hiring in-house.
